The founder

I spent years in influencer marketing.
Watching creators get left behind.

I started in talent agencies, then moved to managing creator campaigns for global brands. For over a decade, I watched the same problem from both sides: creators with genuinely great content, sending emails into the void. Brands with real PR budgets, struggling to find the right people.

The tools that existed were built for agencies — expensive, complicated, and completely out of reach for a solo creator with 8,000 followers who just wanted to pitch Rhode Skin.

So I built the tool I wish existed. Something a nano creator could pick up in 5 minutes — that handles the brand contacts, writes the pitch, attaches the media kit, and keeps track of every outreach. No agency needed. No guessing.

newcollab is still early. I'm one person, building in public, shipping fast. If something's broken, I'll fix it. If you have a brand to add, tell me. This is a product built with its users — not launched at them.

Every creator deserves a real shot at brand deals —
not just the ones who know the right people.

That's what newcollab is for. Contacts, pitch, media kit, tracking — everything a creator needs to reach brands professionally, in one place.

Who this is for

Built for nano and micro creators.
Not for everyone.

newcollab isn't trying to serve every creator at every stage. It's built for one specific person.

This is for you if...

You're a creator who wants brand deals and doesn't know where to start.

  • You create content on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube or a blog
  • You have between 1,000 and 100,000 followers
  • You want PR packages and paid collabs — not just gifted posts
  • You're ready to reach out to brands but have no system
  • You've DMd brands before and got no reply
  • You don't have a media kit and don't know where to start
This probably isn't for you if...

You're an agency, a mega-influencer, or a brand looking to run campaigns.

  • You have a full team managing brand relationships
  • You already have a talent manager or agent
  • You're a brand looking to source creators (not pitch them)
  • You're looking for a full CRM with workflow automation
  • You have 500K+ followers and brands already come to you

What newcollab is
