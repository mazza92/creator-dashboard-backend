## Why This Works

A follow-up from `team@newcollab.co` carries institutional weight a creator's personal email doesn't. It signals:
- This creator is on a professional platform
- Someone is actively managing their outreach
- The brand is being contacted by a service, not just an individual

It also directly solves your core problem: reply before the limit = conversion. This feature creates that reply for Pro users who aren't getting one organically.

## One Risk to Manage Now

**Volume cap is critical.** If this stays manual (you sending the emails), you need a hard limit before it ships:

- 2 bumps per Pro user per month maximum
- Queue displayed to you in your admin dashboard: "5 bumps pending — send today"
- No bump available until 7 days after the pitch was sent (so you're not emailing brands day 1)

At 10 Pro users × 2 bumps = 20 emails/month max — totally manageable manually. At 50 Pro users that's 100 emails/month — still doable but you'll want to semi-automate the template by then.

Add a "Bumps pending" widget to your admin dashboard now so you can action them same-day.
